Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does CallOut share my information? Will my info stay private?
A: Yep! CallOut doesn’t sell any of your information, or share it in any other way—the safety of our customers is our #1 priority, and we along with that comes a value for your privacy.


Q: Is CallOut free?
A: CallOut is a non-profit organization, and is thus 100% free of charge; we exist solely to make your lives better.

Q: What’s the point of CallOut if I can just dial 911?
A: Not every situation is extreme enough to warrant a call to the police. That’s why CallOut is mainly intended to cater to situations where you’re not in any immediate danger. By doing this, we give you a much easier solution to milder issues and make sure the authorities are free to respond to any real emergencies.
