About Us

At CallOut, we know that people’s lives aren’t movies; not every moment is perfectly choreographed and conveniently scripted, and oftentimes interactions are stiff and unnatural. That’s why we’re committed to making every day a just bit easier by giving people a tool to avoid the awkward interactions that can turn elegant occasions into embarrassing ones. Our goal as a company is to ensure that our clients are never afraid of any situation and can savor the great moments in life, while cutting out the ones that aren’t.
